About Aqua.

Who we are, what we're doing, and how we got started.

Jonathan Smith is the CEO and co-founder of Aqua. inc. He developed the Stream. Read a story about him and the Stream here. He created Stream to try to help his pet axolotl Axy have a more comfortable home. “Axy would mostly stay in the opposite corner of the filter,” Smith said. “And when it turned on, he would start swimming around in circles really fast.” The Stream made the water calmer, allowing Axy to live in peace. It has been tested by the University of Kentucky and was featured at the Seattle Mini Maker Faire.
Benjamin is the COO and co-founder of Aqua. inc, as well as the lead hardware and software engineer. He is also the webmaster. He joined Aqua. when his friend Jonathan asked for help developing Auto, and they've worked together ever since.
Aqua's goal is to innovate in the field of aquatic pet care. We strive to create products that make your and your fish's life easier.
